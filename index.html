**Troubleshooting Tip:** If you encounter "Load failed" errors, try using the Google Sheets `gviz/tq` export URL. To get this URL: 1. Find your Sheet ID from your Google Sheet's URL (e.g., `https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/YOUR_SHEET_ID/edit#gid=0`). 2. Find your Sheet Name (the tab name, e.g., `Sheet1`). 3. Construct the URL: `https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/YOUR_SHEET_ID/gviz/tq?tqx=out:csv&sheet=YOUR_SHEET_NAME` This format is often more reliable for programmatic access due to CORS policies.
